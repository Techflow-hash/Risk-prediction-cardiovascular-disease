About Heart Disease Prediction System

What is Heart Disease?

Heart disease refers to various conditions that affect the heart and blood vessels. It remains one of the leading causes of death globally, with an estimated 17.9 million lives taken each year.

Our Prediction System

This advanced system utilizes state-of-the-art machine learning algorithms to predict the likelihood of heart disease based on various health parameters. The prediction accuracy is enhanced through multiple classification algorithms working in conjunction.

Machine Learning Algorithms Used

  • Decision Trees - For pattern recognition in medical data
  • Random Forest - Ensemble learning for improved accuracy
  • K-Nearest Neighbors (KNN) - Pattern matching with existing cases
  • AdaBoost - Adaptive boosting for better predictions
  • Gradient Boosting - Advanced technique for prediction refinement

Key Health Parameters Analyzed

  • Age and Gender
  • Blood Pressure Levels
  • Cholesterol Measurements
  • Blood Sugar Levels
  • Heart Rate Variations
  • ECG Results
  • Exercise Induced Angina
  • Number of Major Vessels
